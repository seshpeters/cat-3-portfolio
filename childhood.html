Family & Early Life

Growing up in a close-knit family, I spent countless hours playing with my siblings and learning from my parents. Our weekend picnics and bedtime stories are memories I cherish deeply.

Formative Moments

My curiosity was sparked early on by building LEGO castles and exploring the outdoors. These experiences taught me creativity, resilience, and the joy of discovery.

Lessons Learned

  • Value of kindness and empathy
  • Importance of teamwork and sharing
  • Never stop asking questions

One of my proudest childhood moments was winning my first school arts competition, which inspired my lifelong love for creativity and self-expression.
